# Project Brief — Needle in a Haystack

**Partner:** IBM (Large company)
**Suitable for:** Big Data and Machine Learning project; students confident in NLP, Data Science, and Data Visualisation.

---

## Overview

This project aims to explore and implement innovative solutions to the
"needle in a haystack" problem—finding rare, relevant information in massive
datasets—using IBM's Granite series of foundation models. By leveraging IBM
Granite's powerful natural language processing (NLP) and retrieval
capabilities, the project will design and evaluate AI-driven systems that
enhance precision in information discovery across enterprise-scale data.

## Background & Importance

Modern organizations are drowning in data—structured, unstructured, and
multimodal. Yet, the challenge lies not in having data, but in extracting the
precise insight needed at the right moment. This is especially critical in
sectors like legal discovery, fraud detection, intelligence analysis,
healthcare diagnostics, and regulatory compliance. Traditional search methods
and basic AI tools often fail to surface rare but critical information. The
ability to locate such "needles" within terabytes of irrelevant or misleading
"hay" has become a competitive and operational imperative.

## Approach

IBM Granite models, designed for enterprise-grade performance, will be
employed to build intelligent retrieval systems capable of understanding
context, intent, and domain-specific nuance. This includes:

- Implementing semantic search and retrieval-augmented generation (RAG)
  pipelines.
- Evaluating performance using precision/recall metrics on benchmark datasets.
- Exploring model explainability and trust in retrieved outputs.

## Value & Impact

This project is high value because it addresses a critical bottleneck in
enterprise knowledge management. A successful solution can dramatically reduce
time-to-insight, mitigate risks, and enhance decision-making. Furthermore, it
showcases the practical potential of foundation models beyond general-purpose
chat—bringing strategic value to industries where precision is non-negotiable.

By demonstrating Granite's capacity to solve one of the most complex
real-world AI challenges, the project not only drives technical innovation but
also positions IBM's AI stack as a differentiator in the emerging era of
intelligent search and retrieval.
